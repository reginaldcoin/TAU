11. The Use of Non-existence.

Thirty spokes unite in one nave, and the hole in the center of it is useful for a carriage wheel.

Earth is molded into vessels, and by their hollowness they are useful as vessels.

Doors and windows are cut out in order to make a house, and by its hollowness it is useful as a house.

So then existence may be said to correspond to gain, but non-existence to use.
