24. Disagreeable Graciousness.

A man on tiptoe cannot stand still.

A man astride his neighbor cannot walk on.

He who is self-displaying does not shine.

He who is self-approving is not held in esteem.

He who is self-praising has no merit.

He who is self-exalting does not stand high.

Such persons are in relation to Tau, as the refuse of food or as excrescences on the body to the creature;
they are universally loathed.

Therefore he who has Tau will not stay where they are.
