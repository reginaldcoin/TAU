42. The Transformation of Tau.

Tau produced one unity;
one unity produced duality;
duality produced trinity;
and trinity produced all things.

Everything carries the yin on its back;
and the yang on its front;
and is harmonized by an intermediate immaterial breath.

What people dislike is orphanage, and loneliness, and a wheelless carriage incompleteness;
and yet kings and nobles appropriate these terms.

For things, when they are diminished, will increase;
and, when they are increased, will diminish.

What people by their behavior teach, I also teach them.

Those, for instance, who are violent and obstinate do not die a natural death.

I would use such as the best instructors.
