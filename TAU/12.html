12. Restraining the Passions.

The five colors will make a man's eyes blind.

The five solids will make a man's ears deaf.

The five tastes will spoil a man's mouth.

Riding and hunting will drive a man mad.

Things hard to procure will make a man run into harm.

Therefore the sage makes provision for the inner man, and not for the eyes.

He puts aside the one, that he may take the other in hand.
