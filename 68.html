68. How to be the Associate of Heaven.

The man who excels as a commander is not warlike.

The man who excels as a fighter is not wrathful.

The man who excels as a conqueror takes no part in war.

The man who excels in employing others places himself below them.

This I call the virtue of not striving.

This I call the power of employing others.

This I call being the associate of Heaven: the supreme aim of antiquity.
