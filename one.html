01. The Embodiment of Tau.

What is observable cannot be Eternal.

All names which can be perceived cannot be Eternal Names.

Non-existence is named the Predecessor of heaven and earth;
and Existence is named the Mother of all things.

In eternal non-existence, therefore, man seeks to pierce the primordial mystery;
and, in eternal existence, to behold the issues of the Universe.

But these two are one and the same, and differ only in name.

This sameness of existence and non-existence I call the mystery within mystery within mystery.
